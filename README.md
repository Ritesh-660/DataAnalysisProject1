# Hotel-Booking-Cancellation-Analysis
Business Problem
Hotel booking cancellations directly impact revenue, forecasting, and resource planning. This project analyzes booking data to identify the key factors influencing cancellations and uncover actionable insights that can support better business decisions.
This project marks the beginning of my Data Analyst journey, where I applied analytical thinking and practical data skills to solve a real-world business problem.

Objective
-> Understand cancellation behavior
->Identify high-risk booking patterns
->Analyze pricing and seasonal trends
->Extract insights that could help reduce revenue loss

Tools & Technologies
->Python
->Pandas & NumPy (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
->Matplotlib & Seaborn (Data Visualization)
->Jupyter Notebook

Analytical Approach
->Performed data cleaning and preprocessing
->Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
->Compared cancellation trends across hotel types
->Analyzed the impact of lead time, pricing (ADR), and seasonality
->Visualized patterns to highlight key business insights

Key Insights
->City hotels experienced significantly higher cancellation rates than resort hotels.
->Longer lead times were strongly associated with increased cancellations.
->Seasonal demand patterns influenced both booking volume and cancellation behavior.
->Pricing trends (ADR) varied across hotel types and peak seasons.
